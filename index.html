How is the Performance Index calculated?

We combine two things — how many points a player has scored, and how good that score is relative to what they cost at auction. A player bought for ₹1 Cr who scores 10 points shouldn't rank above someone bought for ₹80 Cr who scores 400 points. So we use a 70/30 weighted formula — performance matters more, but value still counts.

Points Score
=
Your points ÷ Highest points scored by any player × 100
e.g. If top scorer has 300 pts and you have 150 → Score = 50%
Value Score
=
Your (points ÷ price) ÷ Best (points ÷ price) ratio × 100
e.g. If best ratio is 10 pts/Cr and yours is 5 → Score = 50%
Performance Index
=
(Points Score × 70%) + (Value Score × 30%)
Higher = better overall performer relative to their price tag
Overperforming (Index ≥ 60%)
As Expected (30–59%)
Underperforming (Below 30%)
⚠️ Note: This index becomes more meaningful after 8–10 matches. Early in the season, one big score can skew rankings significantly.
