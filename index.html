How is the Performance Index calculated?

We combine how many points a player scored with how good that score is relative to their auction price. A 70/30 weighted formula — performance matters more, but value still counts. Value score is capped at 15× to prevent very cheap players from dominating.

Points Score
=
Your points ÷ Highest points scored by any player × 100
Value Score
=
Your (points ÷ price) ÷ Best (points ÷ price) ratio × 100 — capped at 15×
Performance Index
=
(Points Score × 70%) + (Value Score × 30%)
